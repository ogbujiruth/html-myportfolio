My top 3 movies of all time

Bel-Air

The captivating journey of Will, a smart kid from philly, who suddendly finds himself living with his wealthy relatives in fancyBel-air, los Angeles. As will tries to find his place in the new world, he faces challenges like fitting in, staying true, and dealing with family dynamics.

Mrs chatterjee vs Norway

The movie is a charming comedy-drama about an Indian woman, Mrs. Chatterjee, who bravely sets out to reunite with her family in Norway. Through a series of misadventures and heartwarming encounters, she navigates bureaucratic challenges and cultural differences, all while showcasing the power of love and determination to overcome obstacles, i actually cried while watching the movie

Alchemy of souls

It's a Korean fantasy film following Hye-jin, who discovers her ability to communicate with spirits. Together with Ji-hoon, they uncover mysteries of the spirit world, confronting their pasts along the way. It's a tale of love, redemption, and the supernatural.
